Island Girl

This island Girl's name is Jessie.
It's short for Justine. Jessie said "My mother named me after my auntie Justine, but I always wished I had been name differently. I like Jessie better. I do like that she is the best around at catching fish from the shore. I want to be like that one day."

She loves the Ocean
You can usually find Justine by the ocean, with her friends, family or one of her 3 dogs "Whisper, Buck or Juniper"
